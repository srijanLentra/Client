Waiver of Premium on CI

In case of diagnosis of any of the critical illnesses listed, future premiums will be waived off & Life cover will continue as usual

Accidental Disability Benefit

In case of an Accidental Permanent Disability, the insured will get 1% of Rider Sum Assured per month for the next 10 years

Critical Illness

If you are diagnosed with a critical illness, lumpsum coverage value will be paid immediately

Cancer Cover

Pays you an additional lumpsum amount if you are diagnosed with Cancer

Accidental Death

In case of death due to an accident, 100% of the rider cover amount shall be payable along with the base coverage

Suicidal Cover

If the Life Insured dies by suicide within 12 months from the Date of commencement of the policy or the revival date of policy, the policy shall terminate immediately. Insurer will refund 80% of the total premiums paid including extra premiums
